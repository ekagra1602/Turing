STEP 1: START THE RECORDER
---------------------------
$ python simple_recorder.py

  Controls:
  - Recording starts IMMEDIATELY
  - Type 'stop' (lowercase) to STOP and save
  - Press ESC to quit without saving


STEP 2: RECORD THE WORKFLOW
----------------------------
The recorder starts automatically! Perform these actions:

  1. Open Brave Browser (via Raycast)
     - Press Option+Space (Raycast)
     - Type "brave browser"
     - Press Enter

  2. Navigate to Workday
     - Cmd+T (new tab) or Cmd+L (address bar)
     - Type "workday.com" (or your company's Workday URL)
     - Press Enter
     - Wait for page to load

  3. Enter Hours
     - Click on time entry section
     - Click on date/day field
     - Enter hours (e.g., "8")
     - Tab or click to next field if needed
     - Enter project/task code if applicable
     - Click Submit/Save button

  4. Type 'stop' to STOP recording
